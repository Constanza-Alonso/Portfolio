EnoTerra

EnoTerra is a wine tourism platform that centralizes winery visits, restaurant reservations, tourism experiences, and wine purchases into a single seamless journey, helping users plan and enjoy their wine travel experience with ease.

Case Study

EnoTerra centralizes winery visits, restaurant reservations, and wine purchases into one seamless journey.

Problem

Wine tourists often struggle to plan their experiences because information about wineries, reservations, dining options, and wine purchases is scattered across multiple websites and platforms.

Goal

Design a unified platform that reduces booking friction by centralizing winery discovery, reservations, and wine purchases into a single seamless experience.

Design Approach

Clear flows, soft visual hierarchy, and practical interaction details.

  1. 01
    Understand

    Mapped user needs, booking friction, and key decision points.

  2. 02
    Structure

    Organized winery content, reservations, and purchase paths.

  3. 03
    Prototype

    Tested key interactions, button states, and section navigation.
